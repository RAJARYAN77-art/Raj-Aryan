WiFi Security Best Practices

  • Use WPA3 encryption when available, or WPA2 as a minimum
  • Create strong passwords with at least 12 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Change default router admin passwords
  • Disable WPS (WiFi Protected Setup) as it has known vulnerabilities
  • Regularly update your router firmware
  • Hide your SSID if you don't need public visibility
  • Use MAC address filtering for additional security
  • Consider using a VPN for sensitive activities on public WiFi
